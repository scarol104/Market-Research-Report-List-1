HC (hydrocarbon) blowing agents are environmentally friendly alternatives that do not harm the ozone layer but may have flammability concerns. HFC (hydrofluorocarbon) blowing agents are another ozone-friendly option with lower flammability and improved performance. Lastly, the "Others" category includes various alternative blowing agents that may possess unique characteristics, such as lower global warming potential or improved thermal stability.</p></p>
