<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The market for electrically adjustable focus lenses includes two main types: electrowetting-based liquid lens and liquid crystal lens. Electrowetting-based liquid lenses use an electric field to manipulate droplets of liquid in order to change the focal length of the lens. This technology allows for quick and precise adjustments. On the other hand, liquid crystal lenses utilize the properties of liquid crystals to alter the lens curvature and thus modify the focal length. Both types of lenses offer flexibility and convenience in focusing, making them highly sought after in various industries.</p></p>
