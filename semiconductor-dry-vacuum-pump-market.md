<p><p>The Semiconductor Dry Vacuum Pump market consists of various types of pumps, including Cam, Claw, Screw, and Others. Cam pumps use rotating blades to create a vacuum, while Claw pumps rely on two rotating claw-shaped rotors. Screw pumps use intermeshing screws to compress air and create a vacuum effect. The 'Others' category includes pumps with different technologies or designs not covered by the aforementioned types. All these pumps find applications in the semiconductor industry, specifically for generating a dry, particle-free vacuum environment required for various manufacturing processes.</p></p>
