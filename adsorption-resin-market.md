<p><p>Adsorption resin is a type of material that is used in various industrial processes to remove impurities from fluids. The market for adsorption resin can be classified into different types based on their composition. Aromatic (Cross-Linked Polystyrenic Matrix) resins are made from cross-linked polystyrene and are ideal for removing organic compounds. Modified Aromatic (Brominated Aromatic Matrix) resins have bromine incorporated into the aromatic matrix, making them effective against heavy metals. Methacrylic (Methacrylic Ester Copolymer) resins are synthesized from methacrylic ester copolymer and are suitable for applications requiring thermal stability. Other types of resins exist, offering unique properties and uses.</p></p>
