<p><p>The steel-plastic composite geogrid market consists of two types: biaxial tension and uniaxial tension. Biaxial tension geogrids are designed to withstand forces from multiple directions, providing stable reinforcement in two perpendicular directions. On the other hand, uniaxial tension geogrids are engineered to withstand forces primarily in one direction, making them suitable for applications where reinforcement is needed in a specific direction. Both types of geogrids are commonly used in civil engineering and construction projects for soil stabilization, slope reinforcement, and pavement improvement.</p></p>
