<p><p>Tyre market types can be categorized into Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM) tyres and replacement tyres. OEM tyres are those that are specifically produced and installed by vehicle manufacturers as original equipment on new vehicles. On the other hand, replacement tyres are purchased by vehicle owners when their original tyres need to be replaced due to wear and tear or damage. OEM tyres are typically more expensive as they are designed to meet specific vehicle requirements, while replacement tyres offer a wider variety of brands and price ranges to cater to different customer preferences and budgets.</p></p>
