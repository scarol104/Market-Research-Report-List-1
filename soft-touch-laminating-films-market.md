<p><p>Soft touch laminating films are a type of laminating material that provide a smooth and velvety texture to the surface they are applied on. These films are available in various types, including BOPP (biaxially oriented polypropylene) film, PET (polyethylene terephthalate) film, and others. BOPP films are made from polypropylene and offer excellent clarity and durability. PET films, on the other hand, are made from polyester and provide high strength and heat resistance. The "Others" category includes laminating films made from different materials, such as PVC (polyvinyl chloride) and nylon.</p></p>
