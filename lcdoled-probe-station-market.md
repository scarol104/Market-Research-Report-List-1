Full-Automatic Probe Stations can automate the entire probing process without human intervention. Semi-Automatic Probe Stations combine manual and automated features, allowing the operator to control certain aspects while automation handles others. These different types cater to varying needs and budgets, offering flexibility and convenience in testing LCD and OLED technologies.</p></p>
