<p><p>The LED Light Diffuser Films market consists of two types: Multi Sheet and Single Sheet. Multi Sheet refers to a film that is composed of multiple layers, each having its own light diffusion properties. This type of film offers enhanced light dispersing characteristics and is commonly used in large-scale lighting applications. On the other hand, Single Sheet refers to a film made from a single layer, providing a more basic level of light diffusion. This type of film is often utilized in smaller lighting fixtures or displays where a lower level of light dispersion is required.</p></p>
