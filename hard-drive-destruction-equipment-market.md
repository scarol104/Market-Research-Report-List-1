<p><p>The hard drive destruction equipment market includes various types of degaussers used to permanently destroy data on hard drives. Coil degaussers use a magnetic coil to generate a strong electromagnetic field that erases data. Capacitive discharge degaussers discharge a high-voltage capacitor through a degaussing coil, effectively destroying the magnetic field of the hard drive. Permanent magnet degaussers use powerful magnets to disrupt the magnetic patterns on the drives, rendering the data unreadable. These different types of degaussers offer different methods to ensure complete data destruction.</p></p>
