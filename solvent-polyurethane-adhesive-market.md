<p><p>Solvent polyurethane adhesive is a type of adhesive that uses solvents to dissolve and bind materials together. There are three main types of solvent polyurethane adhesives: polyether type, polyester type, and mixed type. </p><p>Polyether type adhesives are known for their flexibility, durability, and resistance to moisture and chemicals. They are commonly used in applications where a strong bond is required, such as automotive and construction industries.</p><p>Polyester type adhesives are known for their high strength and resistance to heat and aging. They are often used in applications that require long-term durability, such as in the manufacturing of furniture and appliances.</p><p>Mixed type adhesives combine the properties of both polyether and polyester types. They provide a balance between flexibility and strength, making them suitable for various applications, such as bonding different materials or repairing cracked surfaces.</p></p>
