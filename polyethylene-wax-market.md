<p><p>Polyethylene wax is a type of synthetic wax that is derived from ethylene, a byproduct of crude oil. It is produced by the polymerization of ethylene under controlled conditions. The increasing demand for polyethylene wax in various end-use industries, such as packaging, coatings, and adhesives, is driving market growth.
