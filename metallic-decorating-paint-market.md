<p><ul><li>Solvent Based</li><li>Water Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The market for metallic decorating paint can be broadly categorized into two types: solvent-based and water-based. Solvent-based metallic decorating paints are made using organic solvents, which evaporate during the drying process, leaving behind a smooth and glossy finish. They offer excellent adhesion, durability, and resistance to weather and abrasion. On the other hand, water-based metallic decorating paints are made using water as the primary solvent. They are eco-friendly, have low VOC emissions, and are easy to clean up. While they may not be as durable as solvent-based paints, they offer good color retention and are safer to use.</p></p>
