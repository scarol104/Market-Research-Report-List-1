<p><p>The steering tie rod market consists of two types: inner tie rod and outer tie rod. Inner tie rods are components that connect the steering rack to the steering knuckle, allowing the wheels to turn. Outer tie rods, on the other hand, link the inner tie rod to the steering knuckle and transmit the steering motion. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring proper steering control and stability of a vehicle. They are essential parts of the steering system and are commonly available in the automotive market.</p></p>
