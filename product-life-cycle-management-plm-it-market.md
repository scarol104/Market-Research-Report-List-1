IoT devices embedded in products can provide real-time data on product performance, usage, and customer feedback, enabling proactive maintenance and continuous improvement.</p><p>Overall, the Product Life-Cycle Management (PLM) IT market is undergoing continuous innovation and adoption of advanced technologies to drive efficiency, enhance collaboration, and improve product development and management processes.</p></p>
