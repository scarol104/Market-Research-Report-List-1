<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The PET crystallizer market consists of two types: hot-air hopper crystallizers and infrared drum (IRD) crystallizers. Hot-air hopper crystallizers use hot air to circulate and crystallize the PET material in a hopper, resulting in improved crystallization and reduced drying time. On the other hand, infrared drum (IRD) crystallizers utilize infrared radiation to heat the material in a rotating drum, achieving efficient crystallization and reduced energy consumption. Both types of crystallizers are widely used in the PET industry for enhancing the quality and efficiency of PET material processing.</p></p>
