<p><p>The epoxy repair mortar market consists of two major types: two-component epoxy mortar and three-component epoxy mortar. Two-component epoxy mortar is a mixture of resin and hardener that requires the addition of an aggregate to form a repair mortar. It is easy to use, offers high adhesion, and provides good resistance to chemicals and heavy traffic. On the other hand, three-component epoxy mortar includes an extra component, usually a filler or pigment, enhancing its properties and making it versatile for different applications such as crack repairs, joint filling, and floor coatings.</p></p>
