<p><strong>The Vehicle EPS Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CEPS Motor</li><li>REPS Motor</li><li>PEPS Motor</li><li>EHPS Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vehicle EPS Motor (Electric Power Steering Motor) market can be classified into several types. The most common types are CEPS Motor, REPS Motor, PEPS Motor, and EHPS Motor. CEPS Motor refers to Column-Mounted Electric Power Steering Motor, which is typically used in compact and mid-size vehicles. REPS Motor stands for Rack-Mounted Electric Power Steering Motor, commonly found in larger vehicles and SUVs. PEPS Motor, also known as Pinion Electric Power Steering Motor, is utilized in smaller and sportier vehicles. Lastly, EHPS Motor represents Electric Hydraulic Power Steering Motor, which is mainly used in heavy-duty vehicles and off-road applications. These different types cater to varying vehicle sizes, performances, and steering requirements.</p></p>
