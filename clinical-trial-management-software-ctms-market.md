<p><p>Clinical Trial Management Software (CTMS) market comprises three main types: Web-based CTMS, Licensed Enterprise (On-premise) CTMS, and Cloud-based (SaaS) CTMS. Web-based CTMS is accessed through a web browser and offers flexibility and accessibility. Licensed Enterprise CTMS is installed on the company's servers, providing control but requiring maintenance and support. Cloud-based CTMS, also known as Software as a Service (SaaS), is hosted on cloud servers, providing ease of use, scalability, and cost-effectiveness. These market types cater to different needs and preferences of organizations involved in clinical trial management.</p></p>
