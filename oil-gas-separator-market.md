<p><p>The Oil & Gas Separator Market consists of two main types: Gravitational and Centrifugal. Gravitational separators rely on the principle of gravity to separate oil and gas from the mixture. They utilize differences in density to achieve separation. On the other hand, centrifugal separators employ high-speed rotational motion to separate the mixture components based on their differing densities. Both types of separators are widely used in the oil and gas industry to improve the efficiency of production processes and ensure the quality of extracted resources.</p></p>
