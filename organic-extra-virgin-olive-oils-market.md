<p><strong>The Organic Extra Virgin Olive Oils Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PDO / DOP</li><li>PGI / IGP</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the context of the organic extra virgin olive oils market, there are three main types of market classifications: PDO/DOP, PGI/IGP, and other markets. PDO (Protected Designation of Origin) or DOP (Denominazione di Origine Protetta) is a certification that guarantees the origin and quality of the product, indicating that the oil is produced, processed, and packaged in a specific geographical area. PGI (Protected Geographical Indication) or IGP (Indicazione Geografica Protetta) certifies that at least one stage of production, processing, or preparation takes place in a particular geographical area while maintaining certain quality characteristics. The other market refers to olive oils that do not possess either PDO or PGI certifications.</p></p>
