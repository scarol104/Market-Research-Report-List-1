<p><p>The gravel cleaner market can be categorized into two types: siphon-style gravel cleaners and electric gravel cleaners. Siphon-style gravel cleaners rely on manual suction to create a siphon that extracts debris and waste from the aquarium substrate. They are simple and cost-effective options for small aquariums. Electric gravel cleaners, on the other hand, utilize electric power to create suction and remove impurities. They are generally more powerful and efficient, suitable for larger aquariums or those with heavy waste accumulation. Both types serve the purpose of maintaining a clean and healthy environment for aquatic animals.</p></p>
