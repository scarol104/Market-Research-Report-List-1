<p><p>The Petrochemical Catalyst market is divided into various types based on their specific functions in the petrochemical industry. FCC Catalyst is utilized in fluid catalytic cracking processes to convert heavy hydrocarbon molecules into lighter and more valuable products. Hydrotreating Catalyst helps in removing impurities and sulfur from crude oil, enabling the production of cleaner fuels. Hydrocracking Catalyst breaks down heavy hydrocarbons into lighter fractions for increased yield of valuable products. Polymerization Catalyst aids in the production of polymers from monomers. Alkylation Catalyst enhances the production of higher-octane gasoline. Reforming Catalyst is used in the production of high-octane gasoline. Additionally, there are other catalysts available in the market catering to diverse petrochemical processes.</p></p>
