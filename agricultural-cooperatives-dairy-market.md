<p><p>Agricultural cooperatives in the dairy industry can be categorized into three main types. Federated cooperatives serve as a collective voice for various agricultural cooperatives, advocating for their interests and providing support in areas such as policy-making, marketing, and research. These different types of cooperatives aim to enhance the overall profitability and sustainability of dairy farming.</p></p>
