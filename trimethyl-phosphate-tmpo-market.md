<p><p>Trimethyl phosphate (TMPO) is an organic compound used as a powerful solvent in various industries. It is commonly categorized into two market types: technical grade and electronic grade. Technical grade TMPO is primarily employed in the production of pesticides, polyurethane foams, and flame retardants, owing to its efficient solvency and reactivity. On the other hand, electronic grade TMPO is of higher purity and free from impurities such as metal ions, making it suitable for use in the microelectronics industry, specifically for cleaning and etching of semiconductors and electronic components.</p></p>
