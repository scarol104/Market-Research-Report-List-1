<p><p>Sticky tape is available in various market types, such as paper sticky tape and fibre-reinforced tape. Paper sticky tape is typically made from a thin layer of paper coated with adhesive, offering a versatile and cost-effective solution for everyday tasks. On the other hand, fibre-reinforced tape consists of a layer of fibreglass strands embedded in a strong adhesive, providing enhanced strength and durability, making it ideal for packaging heavy or valuable items. Both market types cater to different needs and preferences, offering adhesive solutions suitable for a wide range of applications.</p></p>
