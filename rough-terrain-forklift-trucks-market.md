<p><p>Rough terrain forklift trucks are specialized vehicles designed to operate on uneven or rugged surfaces. The market for such forklift trucks can be categorized into two types: reach forklifts and mast type forklifts. Reach forklifts are equipped with extendable arms that allow them to reach high heights while maintaining stability. They are commonly used in narrow spaces or warehouses. On the other hand, mast type forklifts have a fixed mast which provides better maneuverability and load capacity. These forklifts are suitable for outdoor applications and rough terrain environments.</p></p>
