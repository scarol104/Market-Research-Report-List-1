<p><p>Additives for the oilfield market are categorized into four main types. Drilling fluid additives refer to chemicals that enhance the performance of drilling mud, increasing its lubricity and preventing well damage. Cementing additives improve the properties of cement used in well construction, ensuring a secure seal and preventing leaks. Stimulation additives are used in hydraulic fracturing to optimize the extraction of oil or gas from the reservoir. Other types of additives include corrosion inhibitors, scale inhibitors, and biocides, which help protect the well and equipment from deterioration and bacterial growth.</p></p>
