<p><ul><li>Micro-Porous Hygiene Films</li><li>Non-Porous Hygiene Films</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hygiene films are categorized into two types: micro-porous hygiene films and non-porous hygiene films. Micro-porous hygiene films are designed with tiny pores that allow water vapor to pass through while preventing liquid penetration, making them suitable for use in breathable and absorbent hygiene products like diapers and sanitary napkins. Non-porous hygiene films, on the other hand, have a dense structure that prevents both liquid and vapor transmission. These films are commonly used in applications where moisture barrier properties are crucial, such as in medical and industrial protective clothing.</p></p>
