<p><p>The cow milking machine market consists of two main types: fully automatic milking machines and semi-automatic milking machines. Fully automatic milking machines are designed to handle the entire milking process without human intervention. These machines can automatically attach and detach from the cows, monitor milk flow, and even clean the udder. On the other hand, semi-automatic milking machines require some manual intervention from the operator. While they can automate certain tasks like milk flow monitoring, the attachment and detachment process and udder cleaning still require human assistance.</p></p>
