<p><p>Hydraulic fluid is a crucial component in hydraulic systems, enabling the transfer of power within machines. There are four common types of hydraulic fluid: mineral oil, synthetic oil, semi-synthetic oil, and bio-based oil. Mineral oil is derived from crude oil and offers adequate lubrication and heat resistance. Synthetic oil is artificially produced, providing superior performance in extreme temperatures and higher loads. Semi-synthetic oil combines mineral and synthetic oils, offering improved lubrication properties and cost-effectiveness. Bio-based oil is made from renewable resources and offers environmental benefits with comparable performance to mineral oil.</p></p>
