<p><p>The industrial tape market consists of several types of tapes for various applications. Non-adhesive industrial tape includes tapes without sticky properties, commonly used for packaging or bundling purposes. Single-sided adhesive tapes have adhesive properties on one side, ideal for joining or sealing surfaces. Double-sided adhesive tapes have adhesive on both sides, ideal for bonding two surfaces together. Transfer tapes are used to transfer adhesive from the tape to another surface. Other types of industrial tapes include specialty tapes like foam tapes, duct tapes, or electrical tapes, each designed for specific purposes.</p></p>
