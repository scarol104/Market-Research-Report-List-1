<p><ul><li>Hot Bond</li><li>Cold Bond</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial rubber repair adhesives are used to repair and bond rubber components in various industries. Two common types of these adhesives are hot bond and cold bond. Hot bond repair involves applying high temperature adhesives that require heating to cure and create a strong bond. This method is suitable for heavy-duty applications with high stress and temperature resistance. Cold bond repair, on the other hand, uses ambient temperature adhesives that require no heat for curing. It is often employed for lighter-duty applications and offers ease of use.</p></p>
