<p><ul><li>Bias Tires</li><li>Radial Tires</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rubber solid tires market consists of two main types: Bias Tires and Radial Tires. Bias Tires are constructed with multiple rubber layers overlapping diagonally from bead to bead, providing a smooth and comfortable ride, while also allowing for good load carrying capacity. On the other hand, Radial Tires have their plies positioned radially across the tire, leading to increased stability and better traction on the road. Both types serve different purposes and cater to different needs in the rubber solid tires market.</p></p>
