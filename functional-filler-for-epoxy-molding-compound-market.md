Microsilica is a fine, amorphous silica powder that enhances the mechanical strength and thermal stability of epoxy compounds. It also improves adhesion to substrates. On the other hand, auxiliary fillers are additional materials that serve specific functions, such as improving electrical insulation, reducing shrinkage, or enhancing flame retardancy. These fillers help optimize the epoxy molding compounds for specific applications.</p></p>
