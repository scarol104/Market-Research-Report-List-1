<p><p>The Casting Current Transformer market includes two main types: Measuring Current Transformers and Protective Current Transformers. Protective Current Transformers, on the other hand, are designed to detect and protect electrical systems from high currents or overload conditions. They provide a secondary circuit with a proportional current output when a fault or excessive current occurs, enabling efficient protection of electrical equipment and preventing potential damage. Both types play an essential role in ensuring the safe and reliable operation of electrical systems.</p></p>
