<p><p>Flexible AC Transmission (FACT) Systems refer to devices that enhance the controllability and stability of electrical power systems. Shunt Compensation is a type of FACT system that manages voltage control and improves system stability by inserting reactive power into the transmission lines. Series Compensation, on the other hand, is a FACT system that enhances transmission line efficiency by inserting series capacitors to reduce line impedance. Lastly, the "Others" category of FACT systems includes devices such as Static VAR Compensators and Unified Power Flow Controllers, which offer a combination of both shunt and series compensation along with additional functions for power flow control and stability enhancement.</p></p>
