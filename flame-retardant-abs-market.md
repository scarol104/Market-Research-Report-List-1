<p><p>Flame retardant ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene) is a type of plastic that is resistant to catching fire. There are two main types of flame retardant ABS in the market. Non-halogen type refers to a formulation that does not contain halogens such as chlorine or bromine. Instead, it relies on other flame retardant mechanisms such as phosphorous or nitrogen compounds. On the other hand, halogen type contains halogens as the main flame retardant component, typically chlorine or bromine compounds. Both types aim to reduce the extent and spread of fire, but they differ in the specific chemical composition used.</p></p>
