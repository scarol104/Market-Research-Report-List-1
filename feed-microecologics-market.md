<p><p>Feed Microecologics refer to the market types of Probiotics, Prebiotics, and Synbiotics in the field of animal feed. Probiotics are live bacteria or yeasts that benefit animal health when ingested, while Prebiotics act as food for these beneficial microorganisms. Synbiotics, on the other hand, are a combination of both Probiotics and Prebiotics, working synergistically to improve gut health and overall animal performance. These market types aim to enhance digestion, nutrient absorption, and overall immune function in animals through the manipulation of their gut microbiota.</p></p>
