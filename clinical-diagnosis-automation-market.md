<p><p>Clinical Diagnosis Automation is a growing market that includes different types of automation systems for medical laboratories. Modular Automation refers to a setup where different modules or components of the diagnostic process are automated separately, allowing flexibility in customization and upgrading. Total Lab Automation, on the other hand, involves a completely automated system where all the steps of the diagnostic process are interconnected and controlled by a central unit, leading to increased efficiency and reduced human error. Both types aim to improve accuracy, workflow, and productivity in clinical laboratories.</p></p>
