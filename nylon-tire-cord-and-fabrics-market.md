<p><ul><li>Bias Tire</li><li>Radial Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nylon tire cord and fabrics market finds application in both bias tire and radial tire markets. In bias tires, the nylon cords are arranged diagonally at an angle across the tire, providing excellent puncture resistance and durability. This design is cost-effective and commonly used in off-road vehicles. On the other hand, radial tires feature nylon cords arranged radially from the center to the sidewall, resulting in improved stability, fuel efficiency, and driving comfort. Radial tires are extensively used in passenger cars and commercial vehicles for their superior performance on paved roads.</p></p>
