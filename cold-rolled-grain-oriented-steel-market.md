<p><p>Cold Rolled Grain Oriented Steel (CRGO) is a type of steel that is commonly used in the production of electrical transformer cores due to its excellent magnetic properties. The market for CRGO can be divided into two main types: coils and sheets. Coils refer to CRGO steel that is in the form of large rolls, commonly used by manufacturers for further processing. Sheets, on the other hand, are the cut and flattened CRGO steel pieces that are ready to be used in transformer core assembly. Both coils and sheets play a crucial role in the CRGO market, catering to the different needs of manufacturers in the electrical industry.</p></p>
