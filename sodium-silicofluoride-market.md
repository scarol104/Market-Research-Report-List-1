<p><p>The Sodium Silicofluoride market consists of two main types: Phosphate Fertilizer Production Method and Neutralization Method. In the Phosphate Fertilizer Production Method, Sodium Silicofluoride is obtained as a byproduct during phosphate fertilizer manufacturing. It is then commonly used as a raw material for the production of fluoride chemicals. On the other hand, in the Neutralization Method, Sodium Silicofluoride is produced by neutralizing fluorosilicic acid with sodium chloride or sodium sulfate. This method is typically used in locations where phosphate fertilizer production is limited.</p></p>
