Sericin is also used in hair care products as it helps in retaining moisture, providing shine, and reducing frizz.</p><p>In the pharmaceutical and healthcare industry, sericin is utilized for its wound-healing properties. It has a hemostatic effect and helps in reducing bleeding and accelerating the wound healing process. Sericin also possesses antioxidant and antibacterial properties, making it useful in the treatment of various skin disorders and infections.</p><p>In the textile industry, sericin plays a significant role in enhancing the dyeability, smoothness, and luster of silk fibers.
