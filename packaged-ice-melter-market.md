<p><p>In the packaged ice melter market, there are two main types of products based on their intended use. The products before snow are designed to be applied as a preventive measure before the snowfall. These ice melters help to prevent the formation of ice and snow accumulation on walkways, driveways, and other outdoor surfaces. On the other hand, products after snow are meant to be used after the snowfall to melt existing ice and snow. These ice melters are effective in removing ice and improving traction on surfaces that are already covered with snow.</p></p>
