<p><p>The extinguishing agents market consists of various types of substances used to control and suppress fires. Halotron extinguishing agents are effective for home and office use as they do not damage sensitive equipment. Foam extinguishing agents work by smothering the fire and preventing oxygen supply. Carbon dioxide extinguishing agents displace oxygen, which quickly extinguishes fires in enclosed spaces. Dry chemical extinguishing agents work by interrupting the chemical reaction of the fire, while wet chemical extinguishing agents are effective for cooking fires. Other types of extinguishing agents include water, clean agent, and powder-based solutions.</p></p>
