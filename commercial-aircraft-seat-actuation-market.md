<p><p>The commercial aircraft seat actuation market can be classified into two types: hydraulic seat actuation systems and electromechanical seat actuation systems. Hydraulic seat actuation systems use hydraulic power to control the movement and positioning of aircraft seats. They are commonly found in older aircraft models. On the other hand, electromechanical seat actuation systems use electric motors and gears to operate the seat movements. These systems are more commonly found in newer aircraft models due to their advantages in terms of weight, reliability, and efficiency.</p></p>
