<p><p>Magnesium hydroxide paste is produced through two different methods: chemical synthesis and physical method. In the chemical synthesis method, magnesium hydroxide is manufactured by reacting magnesium sulfate with sodium hydroxide. This method results in a highly pure and controlled product. On the other hand, the physical method involves the grinding and mixing of magnesium hydroxide powder with a liquid. While this method is simpler and cheaper, the resulting product may have impurities and variations in quality. Both methods cater to the demand for magnesium hydroxide paste in different industries.</p></p>
