<p><p>The forage mower market consists of various types of machinery used for cutting and harvesting forage crops. Rotary mowers are widely used and have blades that rotate horizontally, providing efficient and versatile grass cutting. Rotating disc-type mowers feature several discs with cutting knives, allowing for high-speed operations and effective cutting. Cylinder type mowers employ a cylinder blade that moves against a fixed blade, delivering precise and clean cuts. Other types of forage mowers include flail mowers, sickle bar mowers, and disc mowers with conditioner, offering different cutting mechanisms and functions.</p></p>
