<p><p>The 5G RF inductors market comprises three main types of inductors: wire wound, film, and multilayer. </p><p>Wire wound type inductors are made by winding a conductive wire around a core material, offering high inductance values and robustness.</p><p>Film type inductors utilize a thin conductive film on an insulating substrate, allowing for compact designs and high-frequency operation.</p><p>Multilayer type inductors consist of multiple layers of conductive material on a ceramic substrate, providing excellent performance at high frequencies and compact form factors.</p><p>Each type exhibits its own unique characteristics, enabling manufacturers to choose the most suitable type for their specific 5G applications.</p></p>
