<p><p>The uncoated paper market is broadly categorized into three types: uncoated groundwood paper, uncoated woodfree paper, and others. Uncoated groundwood paper is made from mechanically ground wood pulp, known for its excellent printability and relative cost-effectiveness. Uncoated woodfree paper, on the other hand, is manufactured using chemically processed wood pulp, offering enhanced durability, smoother surface, and higher quality printing. The "others" category includes specialty papers like colored papers, recycled papers, and parchment papers, catering to specific niche markets with unique requirements. These market types play a crucial role in fulfilling diverse needs across various industries.</p></p>
