<p><p>Polyurethane waterproof paint is available in two main market types, namely crystalline and water emulsion. Crystalline polyurethane waterproof paint forms a clear, hard, and durable finish that adheres to the surface, providing protection against water infiltration. On the other hand, water emulsion polyurethane waterproof paint is a water-based solution that creates a flexible, yet watertight coating. It can easily be applied on various surfaces, such as concrete and wood, offering excellent waterproofing properties. Both types of polyurethane waterproof paint are widely used in construction and industrial applications to prevent water damage and enhance structural integrity.</p></p>
