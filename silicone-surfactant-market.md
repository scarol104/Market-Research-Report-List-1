Defoaming agents, on the other hand, reduce or eliminate foam formation. Wetting agents enhance the spreading and wetting capabilities of liquids on solid surfaces. Dispersants aid in dispersing solid particles into a liquid medium. Lastly, the "others" category encompasses other specialized functions like anti-static agents and lubricants.</p></p>
