<p><p>The pesticide technical medicine market consists of three major types: insecticides, herbicides, and fungicides. Insecticides are chemicals used to control and eliminate insects that pose a threat to crops, livestock, or human health. Herbicides, on the other hand, are designed to target and kill unwanted plants, commonly known as weeds. Fungicides, as the name suggests, are substances used to prevent or eradicate fungal diseases, which can negatively impact the growth and health of crops. These three types of pesticides cater to different purposes in agricultural practices to ensure optimal crop protection and yield.</p></p>
