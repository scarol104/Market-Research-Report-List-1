<p><p>Die cutting equipment refers to various machines used in the manufacturing industry for cutting materials into desired shapes. Clicker presses are commonly used for cutting soft materials and are manually operated, using a flat die and a downward pressing motion. Rotary die cutters, on the other hand, are automated machines that use a cylindrical die and rotating motion to cut materials like paper, cardboard, and plastic. Both types have their specific advantages and applications in different industries, depending on the material and shape requirements.</p></p>
