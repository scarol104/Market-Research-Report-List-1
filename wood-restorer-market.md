<p><p>The wood restorer market can be categorized into two main types: water-based wood restorer and oil-based wood restorer. Water-based wood restorers are formulated with water as their main component, making them environmentally friendly and easy to clean up. They work by penetrating the wood and restoring its natural color and shine. On the other hand, oil-based wood restorers are made with oil as their primary ingredient. They provide a deep and long-lasting restoration, protecting the wood from moisture and enhancing its natural beauty. Both types of wood restorers offer effective solutions for rejuvenating and preserving wooden surfaces.</p></p>
