<p><p>The wet process phosphoric acid market is composed of three main types: the nitric acid method, the hydrochloric acid method, and the sulfuric acid method. In the nitric acid method, phosphoric acid is produced by reacting phosphate rock with nitric acid. The hydrochloric acid method involves reacting phosphate rock with hydrochloric acid to obtain phosphoric acid. Lastly, the sulfuric acid method produces phosphoric acid by reacting phosphate rock with sulfuric acid. These different methods offer varying production processes, costs, and end-product qualities within the wet process phosphoric acid market.</p></p>
