<p><p>The non-fluorinated water repellent market is categorized into two main types: solvent-based and water-based. Solvent-based repellents are formulated using organic solvents as the main carrier for the active ingredients. They provide excellent water repellency but may pose health and environmental risks. On the other hand, water-based repellents use water as the primary carrier, making them more environmentally friendly and safer for users. While they may not offer the same level of water repellency as solvent-based options, water-based repellents are gaining popularity due to their sustainable nature.</p></p>
