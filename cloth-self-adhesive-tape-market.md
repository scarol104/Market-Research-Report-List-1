On the other hand, coated cloth self-adhesive tape has a layer of coating applied to its surface, providing additional strength, durability, and resistance to moisture and other environmental factors. This type of tape is typically used in more demanding applications, including heavy-duty bundling, sealing, and repairs.</p></p>
