<p><p>Wheat seeds market can be categorized into two main types: Winter Wheat Seed and Spring Wheat Seed. Winter Wheat Seed is planted in the fall and requires a cold season to stratify, which enhances its ability to withstand harsh winter conditions. It typically has higher yields and is harvested in early summer. On the other hand, Spring Wheat Seed is sown in the spring and grows during the warmer months, resulting in a shorter growing cycle and lower yields compared to winter wheat. Both types contribute significantly to the global wheat production.</p></p>
