<p><p>Polyvinyl chloride (PVC) resins are materials used in various applications, including construction, automotive, and packaging industries. There are three main types of PVC resins: suspension, emulsion, and blended. Suspension PVC resins are made through a suspension polymerization process, resulting in a rigid and durable material suitable for pipes and bottles. Emulsion PVC resins are produced using an emulsion polymerization method, creating a flexible and soft material used for coatings and films. Blended PVC resins combine both suspension and emulsion methods to achieve specific properties suitable for different applications.</p></p>
