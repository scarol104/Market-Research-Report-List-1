<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Self-Organizing Network (SON) market can be categorized into three types: D-SON, C-SON, and Hybrid SON. D-SON, or Distributed SON, focuses on distributing network management functions across different network elements. C-SON, or Centralized SON, centralizes network management functions in a single node or element. Hybrid SON combines elements of both D-SON and C-SON, utilizing a combination of distributed and centralized network management. These different SON market types cater to various network management needs and preferences, allowing operators to choose the approach that best suits their requirements.</p></p>
