<p><p>Contact adhesives, also known as contact cement or rubber cement, are adhesives that are applied to both surfaces being joined together. Unlike other adhesives, contact adhesives do not require pressure or clamping to bond the surfaces. Once the adhesive is applied, it needs to dry before the surfaces can be brought together. Contact adhesives are commonly used in construction, automotive, woodworking, and DIY projects.</p><p>The future outlook for the contact adhesives market is promising, with a projected CAGR of 12.2% during the forecast period.
