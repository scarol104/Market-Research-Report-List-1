<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The commercial vehicle airbag inflator market consists of three types: pyrotechnic inflators, stored gas inflators, and hybrid inflators. Pyrotechnic inflators use a small explosion to generate gas for airbag inflation. Stored gas inflators store compressed gas to inflate the airbag during an accident. Hybrid inflators combine elements of both pyrotechnic and stored gas inflators to provide a controlled and efficient airbag deployment. These three types cater to the specific needs of commercial vehicles, ensuring enhanced safety in case of collisions or accidents.</p></p>
