<p><p>The solar micro inverter market consists of two main types: grid-connected solar micro inverters and grid-off solar micro inverters. Grid-connected solar micro inverters are designed to be connected to the electrical grid, allowing excess generated solar energy to be transferred back to the grid for credit or used by other consumers. On the other hand, grid-off solar micro inverters are used in off-grid systems, where there is no connection to the electrical grid, and the generated solar energy is stored in batteries for later use.</p></p>
