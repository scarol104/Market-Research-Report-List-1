<p><ul><li>Slip Sheets</li><li>Tier Sheets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Slip sheets and tier sheets are market types used in the logistics and materials handling industry. Slip sheets are thin, flat sheets made of plastic or cardboard that are used instead of wooden pallets to move and transport goods. They are slipped beneath the load and then pulled out, leaving the load supported by its own weight. Tier sheets, on the other hand, are rigid boards or sheets used to separate layers of products within a pallet, providing stability and organization. Both slip sheets and tier sheets offer cost savings, efficiency, and space optimization in the transportation of goods.</p></p>
