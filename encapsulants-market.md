<p><p>Encapsulants are materials used to protect and insulate electronic devices from environmental factors. The market for encapsulants can be categorized into three types: room temperature cure, heat temperature cure, and UV cure. Room temperature cure encapsulants are cured at ambient temperature, offering convenience and cost-effectiveness. Heat temperature cure encapsulants require higher temperatures for curing, providing improved adhesion and mechanical properties. UV cure encapsulants use ultraviolet light for curing, offering rapid and precise curing with minimal heat generation. Each type has its own advantages and is suitable for specific applications, depending on the desired properties and requirements.</p></p>
