<p><p>Car active headrests are safety features installed in cars to prevent whiplash injuries during accidents. There are two types of car active headrests on the market, namely headrest activation and seat back activation. In headrest activation, the headrests are independently activated upon impact, moving forward to support the head and neck. On the other hand, seat back activation involves the entire seat back moving forward to provide support. Both types function to reduce the risk of whiplash injuries by minimizing the movement of the head and neck during a collision.</p></p>
