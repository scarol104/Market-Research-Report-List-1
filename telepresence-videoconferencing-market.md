<p><p>Telepresence, specifically videoconferencing, can be classified into two main market types: point-to-point and multiple participants. Point-to-point refers to a videoconferencing setup that involves a connection between two parties at separate locations. It allows for direct communication and collaboration between these two participants. On the other hand, the multiple participants market allows for videoconferencing sessions with more than two participants. In this scenario, individuals or groups from various locations can join a single video call simultaneously, facilitating virtual meetings, discussions, and presentations with broader engagement.</p></p>
