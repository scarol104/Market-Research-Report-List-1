<p><p>Radio Thin Layer Chromatography (TLC) scanner is a device used to analyze and quantify radioactive compounds separated by TLC. There are two types of collimators available in the market for these scanners: Dual Collimator and Single Collimator. </p><p>Dual Collimator scanners use two detectors to simultaneously measure two radioactive compounds on the same TLC plate, offering a higher throughput and increased efficiency. On the other hand, Single Collimator scanners have only one detector, allowing analysis of a single radioactive compound at a time. Both types offer accurate quantification and analysis of radioactive compounds separated by TLC, but with different throughput capabilities.</p></p>
