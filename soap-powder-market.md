<p><p>The soap powder market can be classified into two main types: non-phosphorus and phosphorus. Non-phosphorus soap powder refers to products that are formulated without the use of phosphorus compounds. These are often considered more environmentally friendly as phosphorous can contribute to water pollution. On the other hand, the phosphorus market includes soap powders that contain phosphorus compounds. While effective in cleaning, these products can have negative environmental impacts if not properly handled or disposed of, as phosphorus can contribute to excessive algae growth in water bodies.</p></p>
