<p><p>Electroplating pretreatment agents are used to prepare metal surfaces for electroplating processes. There are three types of market categories based on the pH levels of these agents. Acidic pretreatment agents have a low pH level and are effective in removing oxides and contaminants from metal surfaces. Neutral pretreatment agents have a balanced pH level and are commonly used for cleaning and activating metal surfaces. Alkalinity pretreatment agents have a high pH level and are useful in removing rust and scale from metal surfaces. Each type of pretreatment agent offers specific benefits for electroplating processes.</p></p>
