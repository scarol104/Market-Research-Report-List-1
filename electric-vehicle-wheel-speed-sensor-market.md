<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Wheel Speed Sensor market consists of two main types: Hall Type and Magnetic Electric Type. Hall Type sensors use Hall effect to measure the wheel speed and provide a digital output signal. These sensors are commonly used in modern vehicles due to their compact size and accurate readings. On the other hand, Magnetic Electric Type sensors utilize magnetic fields to detect the wheel rotation and generate an electrical signal. These sensors are primarily found in older vehicles and offer a reliable and cost-effective solution for monitoring wheel speed.</p></p>
