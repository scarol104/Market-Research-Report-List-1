<p><p>Neuro stimulation devices market comprises different types of devices used for various medical purposes. DBS (Deep-Brain Stimulation) devices are implanted in the brain to treat neurological disorders such as Parkinson's disease. VNS (Vagus Nerve Stimulation) devices are used to stimulate the vagus nerve and control seizures in epilepsy patients. RNS (Responsive Neuro Stimulation) devices detect abnormal brain activity and provide responsive electrical stimulation. TMS (Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation) devices use magnetic fields to stimulate brain nerve cells for treating depression. SCS (Spinal Cord Stimulation) devices deliver electric current to the spinal cord to alleviate chronic pain. PNS (Peripheral Nerve Stimulation) devices target peripheral nerves for pain relief.</p></p>
