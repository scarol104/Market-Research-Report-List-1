Manual welding machines require human intervention at every step of the welding process. Semi-automatic welding machines combine human control with some automated functions, making the process more efficient. Automatic welding machines are fully automated, eliminating the need for human intervention, resulting in higher productivity and precision. Each type caters to different welding requirements, offering varying levels of control, efficiency, and automation in the welding process.</p></p>
