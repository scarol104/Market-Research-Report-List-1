<p><p>PVDC (Polyvinylidene Chloride) is a polymer made from vinylidene chloride monomers. It is widely used for its excellent barrier properties against moisture, oxygen, and other gases. PVDC is available in two market types - PVDC Latex and PVDC Resins. PVDC Latex is a water-based dispersion used in the coating of films and paper for packaging applications. On the other hand, PVDC Resins are solid materials used in the production of films, sheets, and other packaging applications that require high barrier properties. Both PVDC Latex and PVDC Resins offer superior protection for various products against external elements.</p></p>
