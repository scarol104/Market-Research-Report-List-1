<p><p>Glass Fiber Nylon, also known as Glass Fiber Reinforced Nylon, is a composite material that combines nylon resin with glass fibers to enhance its strength, rigidity, and dimensional stability. It is widely used in various industries, such as automotive, electrical & electronics, and aerospace. Long Glass Fiber Reinforced Nylon consists of glass fibers with a length ranging from 3 to 25 mm, providing high strength and impact resistance. On the other hand, Short Glass Fiber Reinforced Nylon contains glass fibers with a length of less than 3 mm, offering improved dimensional stability and reduced shrinkage. Both types have distinct applications based on their specific properties.</p></p>
