<p><p>The Electronic Straight Hair Combs market can be classified into three types based on the heating mechanism they use: Ceramic Heater, PTC Heater, and Heating Wire Heater. Ceramic Heater combs utilize a ceramic element to produce heat evenly, reducing the chances of heat damage to the hair. PTC Heater combs use a positive temperature coefficient element that quickly heats up and maintains a constant temperature while preventing overheating. Heating Wire Heater combs, as the name suggests, employ a heating wire to generate heat efficiently. These different types offer consumers a range of options based on their preferences and haircare needs.</p></p>
