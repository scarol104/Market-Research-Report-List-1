<p><p>A gravity separator is a mechanical separation device that utilizes the difference in specific gravities of different materials to separate them. This process enables the separation of solids and liquids or different solid materials based on their specific gravity.</p><p>The gravity separator market is projected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
