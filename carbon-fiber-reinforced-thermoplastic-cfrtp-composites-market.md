<p><p>Carbon Fiber Reinforced Thermoplastic (CFRTP) composites are advanced materials that combine carbon fiber with a thermoplastic matrix. There are two main types of CFRTP composites: Polyacrylonitrile (PAN)-based CFRTP and Pitch-based CFRTP. PAN-based CFRTP composites are derived from polyacrylonitrile fibers, which undergo a carbonization process to create strong carbon fibers. On the other hand, Pitch-based CFRTP composites are derived from coal tar pitch, which is heated to obtain carbon fibers. Both types of CFRTP composites offer excellent strength, stiffness, and lightweight properties, making them suitable for various applications in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and sports equipment.</p></p>
