<p><p>Wireless automotive communication technology encompasses various market types, including DSRC (Dedicated Short Range Communication) and Mesh networks. DSRC refers to a technology that enables short-range communication between vehicles and infrastructure, allowing them to exchange critical information for improved safety and efficiency. On the other hand, Mesh networks establish a decentralized wireless network among vehicles, enabling them to communicate directly with each other. This peer-to-peer communication system enhances safety by providing real-time updates regarding road conditions, traffic congestion, and other relevant data, facilitating smoother traffic flow and accident prevention.</p></p>
