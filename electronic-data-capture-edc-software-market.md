<p><p>Electronic Data Capture (EDC) Software market involves two types: On-premise and Cloud-based. On-premise EDC software is installed locally on servers and managed by the user's IT team. This offers complete control and security, but incurs higher setup and maintenance costs. On the other hand, cloud-based EDC software is hosted remotely by a third-party provider and accessed through the internet. It provides flexibility, scalability, and cost-efficiency as users can access and manage their data from anywhere. However, data security and reliance on internet connectivity are potential concerns.</p></p>
