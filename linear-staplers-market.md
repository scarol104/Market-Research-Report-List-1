<p><p>The Linear Staplers Market is segmented into Type I and Type II. Type I linear staplers are typically used in surgical procedures involving thick tissue and require a single firing to close and cut the tissue. On the other hand, Type II linear staplers are designed for thinner tissues, requiring two firings to seal and cut the tissue effectively. These market types indicate the different specifications and functionalities of linear staplers based on tissue thickness, enabling medical professionals to choose the appropriate type for specific surgical needs.</p></p>
