<p><p>The sodium sulphide market comprises three types: industrial sodium sulphide, low ferric sodium sulphide, and anhydrous sodium sulphide. Industrial sodium sulphide is widely used in various industries such as paper manufacturing, textile, oil refining, and leather. Low ferric sodium sulphide is a purer form of sodium sulphide, commonly used in the production of dyes and pigments. Anhydrous sodium sulphide is a dehydrated form, primarily used in the mining industry for ore flotation. Each type serves specific industrial purposes, contributing to the diverse applications of sodium sulphide in the market.</p></p>
