<p><ul><li>Rigid Heat Shield</li><li>Flexible Heat Shield</li><li>Textile Heat Shield</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car heat shields market consists of three types: rigid heat shields, flexible heat shields, and textile heat shields. Rigid heat shields are made of solid materials such as aluminum and are designed to provide maximum protection against heat transfer. Flexible heat shields, on the other hand, are made of lightweight materials like stainless steel or titanium and can be easily bent or shaped to fit various vehicle parts. Textile heat shields are made of insulating materials like fiberglass and are used to reduce heat radiation without adding excessive weight to the vehicle.</p></p>
