<p><p>The toxic gas monitor market is categorized into two types: fixed and portable. Fixed gas monitors are stationary devices that are permanently installed in a specific location, typically in industrial settings. They continuously monitor the surrounding air for the presence of toxic gases and raise alarms if dangerous levels are detected.
