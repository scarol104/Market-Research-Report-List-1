SBS (Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene) is widely used in applications like adhesive and sealants, as it offers excellent tackiness and good heat resistance. SIS (Styrene-Isoprene-Styrene) is commonly employed in elastic materials like hot-melt adhesives, owing to its excellent flexibility and elongation at low temperatures. SEBS (Styrene-Ethylene/Butylene-Styrene) is known for its elastomeric properties, making it suitable for applications like molded rubber goods. SEPS (Styrene-Ethylene/Propylene-Styrene) offers enhanced chemical resistance, making it suitable for applications in harsh environments.</p></p>
