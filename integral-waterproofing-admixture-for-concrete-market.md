<p><p>Integral waterproofing admixture for concrete is used to enhance the durability and waterproofing properties of concrete structures. There are three types of integral waterproofing admixture: Crystalline type, Pore Blocking type, and Other type. </p><p>The Crystalline type admixture works by creating a network of insoluble crystals within the concrete, which block the pathways for water penetration. Pore Blocking type admixture fills the capillary pores of concrete, reducing water absorption. The Other type includes admixtures that function through various mechanisms such as hydrophobic materials or modifying agents, enhancing the waterproofing performance of concrete.</p></p>
