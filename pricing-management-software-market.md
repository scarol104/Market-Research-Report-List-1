<p><p>Pricing management software has two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and easy remote access. It is cost-effective as it eliminates the need for on-site hardware and maintenance. On the other hand, on-premises software is installed locally on a company's servers and requires physical infrastructure and IT support. It provides more control and security but may involve higher upfront costs and limited accessibility outside the company's premises.</p></p>
