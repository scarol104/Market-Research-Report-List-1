<p><p>The elastomer market consists of three main types: rubber, plastic, and silica gel. Rubber elastomers are flexible materials that exhibit high resilience and elasticity, commonly used in tires, automotive components, and seals. Plastic elastomers, also known as thermoplastic elastomers (TPE), combine the properties of rubber and plastics, offering flexibility, high tensile strength, and processability. They find applications in consumer goods, medical devices, and electrical cables. Silica gel, a porous and synthetic material, is mainly used as a desiccant due to its moisture-absorbing properties, finding applications in packaging, electronics, and preservation industries.</p></p>
