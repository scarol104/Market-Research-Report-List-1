<p><strong>The Wireless Stylus Pen Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Type</li><li>Passive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless stylus pen market can be categorized into two types: active type and passive type. Active stylus pens have an internal power source and require a device with an active digitizer to function properly. They offer a high level of precision and responsiveness, making them suitable for professional use. On the other hand, passive stylus pens do not have an internal power source and rely on the device's capacitive touchscreen for operation. They are more affordable and easier to use but may have limited accuracy and responsiveness compared to the active type.</p></p>
