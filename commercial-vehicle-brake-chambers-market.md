<p><p>Commercial vehicle brake chambers are essential components of commercial vehicles' braking systems. They are responsible for converting air pressure into mechanical force to apply the brakes. There are three main types of brake chambers in the market. Piston type brake chambers use a piston to transfer the air pressure and are suitable for heavy-duty vehicles. Diaphragm type brake chambers employ a flexible diaphragm to generate the necessary force and are commonly used in medium-duty vehicles. Other types include wedge type, spring brake chambers, etc., which offer unique features and advantages based on specific vehicle requirements.</p></p>
