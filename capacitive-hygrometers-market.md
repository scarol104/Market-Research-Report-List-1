<p><strong>The Capacitive Hygrometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Relative Type</li><li>Absolute Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Capacitive hygrometers are devices used to measure the humidity levels in the surrounding environment. There are two main types of capacitive hygrometers: relative type and absolute type. </p><p>The relative type measures the humidity based on the ratio of water vapor in the air to its maximum capacity at a given temperature. It provides a percentage value that indicates the relative humidity.</p><p>On the other hand, the absolute type measures the exact concentration of water vapor in the air, typically expressed in grams of water vapor per cubic meter. It provides a more precise measurement of humidity levels.</p><p>Both types are widely used in various industries, including agriculture, meteorology, and HVAC systems, to monitor and control humidity levels for optimal performance and comfort.</p></p>
