<p><p>The silicone implant market consists of two types - silicone filling and saline filling. Silicone filling implants are composed of a silicone outer shell filled with a silicone gel, offering a more natural feel and appearance. They are commonly used for breast augmentation and reconstruction surgeries. On the other hand, saline filling implants utilize a silicone outer shell filled with a sterile saline solution. They are often preferred for their safety profile and ease of adjustment during surgery. Both types serve as options for individuals seeking breast enhancement or reconstruction.</p></p>
