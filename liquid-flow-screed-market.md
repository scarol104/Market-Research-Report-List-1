<p><strong>The Liquid Flow Screed Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pumpable Calcium Sulphate Screed</li><li>Pumpable Cement Based Screed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid flow screed market consists of two main types: pumpable calcium sulphate screed and pumpable cement-based screed. Pumpable calcium sulphate screed is a self-leveling screed that is made from calcium sulphate binder, sand, water, and additives. It is known for its fast drying time and thermal conductivity. On the other hand, pumpable cement-based screed is made from cement binder, sand, water, and additives. It is primarily used for underfloor heating systems due to its high thermal conductivity and strength. Both types offer efficient and reliable solutions for various construction projects.</p></p>
