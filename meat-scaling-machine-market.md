<p><p>The meat scaling machine market consists of two main types: digital kitchen weighing scales and mechanical kitchen weighing scales. Digital scales use electronic sensors to measure the weight of meat accurately and display it on a digital screen. They often come with additional features such as tare function and unit conversion. Both types of scales serve the purpose of accurately measuring the weight of meat during the preparation process.</p></p>
