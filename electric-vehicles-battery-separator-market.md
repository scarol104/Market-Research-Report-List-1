<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Electric Vehicles (EVs) Battery Separator Market is categorized into three types: Single-layer, Bilayer, and Trilayer. Single-layer separators are made of polypropylene (PP) or polyethylene (PE). Bilayer separators consist of a combination of PP/PE or PP/PP. Trilayer separators are composed of PP/PE/PP. Each type serves as a physical barrier between the positive and negative electrodes in an EV's battery, preventing short circuits while allowing the flow of ions. These separators play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and efficiency of the battery system in electric vehicles.</p></p>
