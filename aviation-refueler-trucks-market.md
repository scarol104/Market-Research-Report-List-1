The growth can also be attributed to the rising demand for fuel-efficient trucks and the modernization of airport infrastructure. Additionally, the increasing focus on reducing emissions and improving fuel efficiency has led to the development of advanced refueler trucks that are more technologically advanced and environmentally friendly.</p><p>The market analysis of the aviation refueler trucks market indicates that North America has the largest market share, followed by Europe, due to the presence of major aircraft manufacturers and expanding aviation industry in these regions.
