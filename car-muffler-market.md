<p><ul><li>Bullet Mufflers</li><li>Chambered Mufflers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car muffler market consists of various types of mufflers, including bullet mufflers and chambered mufflers. Bullet mufflers feature a straight-through design with a perforated tube and sound-absorbing material. They offer improved exhaust flow and a deep, aggressive sound. On the other hand, chambered mufflers use multiple chambers and baffles to reduce noise and create a more mellow sound. They are preferred by those seeking a quieter driving experience. Both muffler types cater to different preferences in terms of sound characteristics and exhaust performance.</p></p>
