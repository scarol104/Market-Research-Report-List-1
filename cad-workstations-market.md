<p><p>CAD workstations are specialized computers used for computer-aided design (CAD) applications. There are two main types of CAD workstations in the market - fixed workstations and mobile workstations. </p><p>Fixed workstations are desktop computers designed for heavy-duty CAD tasks. They have powerful processors, large amounts of memory, and dedicated graphics cards, allowing for efficient processing and rendering of complex designs. Fixed workstations are typically used in offices or studios where mobility is not a priority.</p><p>On the other hand, mobile workstations are laptops specifically designed for CAD professionals who require mobility. They offer similar capabilities to fixed workstations, including high-performance components and robust graphics, while also providing the flexibility to work from different locations, such as construction sites or client meetings. Mobile workstations provide on-the-go accessibility without compromising computational power.</p></p>
