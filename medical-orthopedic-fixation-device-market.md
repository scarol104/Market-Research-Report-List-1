<p><strong>The Medical Orthopedic Fixation Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Fixation Device</li><li>Internal Fixation Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical orthopedic fixation device market consists of two main types: external fixation devices and internal fixation devices. External fixation devices are used to stabilize fractured bones by attaching metal pins or screws externally, providing support and allowing for bone healing. On the other hand, internal fixation devices are implanted surgically to stabilize fractured bones internally using devices such as plates, screws, and rods. Both types of devices play a crucial role in the treatment of orthopedic injuries and help promote faster healing and improved mobility.</p></p>
