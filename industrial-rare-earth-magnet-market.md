<p><p>The Industrial Rare Earth Magnet Market can be categorized into three main types: rare-earth sintered magnet, rare-earth bonded magnet, and other markets. Rare-earth sintered magnets are manufactured using a powdered mixture that is pressed and then sintered into the desired shape. Rare-earth bonded magnets, on the other hand, are made by combining magnetic powder with a binder, forming a flexible or rigid magnet. The "other" market refers to various applications of rare-earth magnets outside of the sintered and bonded magnet sectors, such as magnetic refrigeration or magnetic resonance imaging systems.</p></p>
