<p><p>Functional coatings are specialized materials that are applied to the surfaces of various products to enhance their performance and protect them from environmental factors. There are three main types of functional coatings in the market. Powder coating involves electrostatically spraying a dry powder onto a product's surface and then heating it to form a durable finish. E-Coat, or electrodeposition coating, is a process in which electrically charged particles are deposited onto a product's surface, providing excellent corrosion resistance. Plating involves electroplating metal onto a product's surface to enhance its appearance and provide protection against wear and corrosion.</p></p>
