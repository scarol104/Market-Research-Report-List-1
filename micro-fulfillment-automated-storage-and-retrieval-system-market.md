<p><p>Micro-fulfillment automated storage and retrieval systems (AS/RS) refer to technology-driven solutions used to optimize the storage and retrieval process in micro-fulfillment centers. In terms of market types, there are two main categories: single-mast and multi-mast systems. Single-mast AS/RS typically consist of a single vertical mast that houses the storage and retrieval mechanism, suitable for smaller operations with lower inventory needs. On the other hand, multi-mast AS/RS employ multiple vertical masts for higher storage capacity and increased throughput, making them ideal for larger-scale micro-fulfillment centers.</p></p>
