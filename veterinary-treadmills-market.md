<p><p>In the veterinary industry, there are two main types of treadmills used for rehabilitation and exercise purposes. Underwater treadmills are designed to provide low-impact workouts for animals, particularly dogs, by utilizing the buoyancy of water to reduce stress on joints and muscles. They are especially useful for rehabilitating injuries or surgeries. On the other hand, standard treadmills are similar to those used by humans, but adjusted for animals. These treadmills allow animals to receive controlled exercise and improve their overall fitness levels. Both types serve as valuable tools in veterinary clinics and rehabilitation centers.</p></p>
