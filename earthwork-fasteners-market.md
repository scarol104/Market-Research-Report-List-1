<p><p>The Earthwork Fasteners market consists of three main types: Bolts, Nuts, and Others. Bolts are threaded cylindrical fasteners that are used to hold objects together or secure them in place. Nuts, on the other hand, are small, usually hexagonal-shaped objects with a threaded hole in the center. They are used in conjunction with bolts to create a secure connection. "Others" refer to various types of fasteners such as screws, washers, and anchor bolts that are also employed in earthwork applications to ensure stability and support.</p></p>
