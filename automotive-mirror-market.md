<p><p>The automotive mirror market comprises two main types: rear-view mirrors and side view mirrors. Rear-view mirrors are positioned inside the vehicle to provide the driver with a clear view of the road behind. They help in gauging the distance of vehicles approaching from the rear. Side view mirrors, located on the exterior of the vehicle, assist in monitoring blind spots and provide a wider field of vision for the driver. Both types of mirrors are essential for safe driving and are often equipped with additional features such as heating and auto-dimming capabilities.</p></p>
