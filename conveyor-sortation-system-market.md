<p><p>A Conveyor Sortation System is used to sort and distribute items efficiently in various industries. Linear Sortation Systems involve a straight conveyor line with multiple diverter stations to divert items onto different takeaway lines. This system is suitable for industries with a high volume of small items. On the other hand, Loop Sortation Systems use a looped conveyor line with mergers and diverters to sort items. This system is ideal for industries with a lower volume but a wider variety of items to be sorted. Both systems improve productivity and accuracy in sorting processes.</p></p>
