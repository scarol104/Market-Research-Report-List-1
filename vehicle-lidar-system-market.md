<p><strong>The Vehicle Lidar System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Lidar</li><li>Solid State Lidar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vehicle Lidar System market can be categorized into two types: Mechanical Lidar and Solid State Lidar. Mechanical Lidar systems use moving physical components, such as rotating mirrors, to scan the environment and generate a 3D map. Solid State Lidar, on the other hand, utilizes solid-state components like micro-electromechanical systems (MEMS) to emit and detect laser beams without moving parts. Both types have their advantages and drawbacks, with Mechanical Lidar offering longer detection distances, while Solid State Lidar provides compactness and durability.</p></p>
