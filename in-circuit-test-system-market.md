<p><p>An In-Circuit Test (ICT) System is an automated electronic test method used to validate the functionality and performance of electronic circuit boards before they are assembled into final products. It checks for any defects or issues in the components and connections on the board. The ICT system applies electrical signals to the board and measures the corresponding responses to determine the circuit's quality. It is widely used in the manufacturing industry for testing printed circuit boards (PCBs) in products such as consumer electronics, automotive, aerospace, and telecommunications.</p><p>The global In-Circuit Test System market is experiencing significant growth due to increasing demand for high-quality electronic products and the rising complexity of circuitry. <p><p>The In-Circuit Test System market is categorized into two types: semi-automatic and fully-automatic systems. Semi-automatic systems require manual intervention during the testing process, where operators are responsible for handling the test probes and initiating the tests. In contrast, fully-automatic systems operate without human intervention, with all testing procedures being automated, including probe handling and test initiation. Both types aim to ensure efficient and accurate testing of electronic circuits, but their level of automation differs.</p></p>
