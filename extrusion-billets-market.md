Additionally, the growing construction industry, particularly in emerging economies, is fueling the demand for aluminum extrusion products, as they are extensively used in building structures, windows, and doors.</p><p>In terms of the latest trends, there is a shift towards the use of advanced alloys and composite materials for extrusion billets. Moreover, advancements in extrusion technology, such as the use of computer-aided design and simulation software, are helping manufacturers optimize the extrusion process to produce complex shapes and reduce material waste.</p><p>To sum up, the extrusion billets market is expected to grow at a steady pace, driven by the increasing demand for lightweight and sustainable materials in various industries.
