<p><p>The organic peroxide market can be categorized into various types. Alkyl hydroperoxide includes peroxides that contain hydrocarbon groups, while dialkyl peroxide refers to a type where two alkyl groups are present. Diacyl peroxide consists of peroxides containing two acyl groups. Peroxide ester includes peroxides formed from the reaction between a peroxy acid and an alcohol. Peroxidation ketal refers to peroxides formed through the reaction between dioxolanes and hydrogen peroxide. Peroxydicarbonate includes peroxides formed from the reaction between carbonate esters and hydrogen peroxide. Lastly, there are other types of organic peroxides not falling into the above categories.</p></p>
