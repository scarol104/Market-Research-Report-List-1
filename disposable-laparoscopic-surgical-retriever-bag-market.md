<p><p>Disposable laparoscopic surgical retriever bags are used in laparoscopic surgeries to remove and retrieve dissected tissue or organs from the abdominal cavity. There are two types of market options available: detachable and non-detachable. Detachable bags are designed with a detachable top, allowing for easy retrieval and removal of the specimen from the bag, minimizing the risk of contamination. Non-detachable bags have a fixed top and are typically used when the specimen can be easily extracted after the bag is removed. Both types offer convenience and efficiency but cater to different surgical requirements.</p></p>
