<p><strong>The RFID Electronic Label Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LF RFID</li><li>HF RFID</li><li>UHF RFID</li><li>MW RFID</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RFID Electronic Label Market includes four main types: LF RFID, HF RFID, UHF RFID, and MW RFID. LF RFID operates at low frequencies, typically below 135 kHz, and is commonly used for access control and animal tracking. HF RFID, with frequencies between 13.56 MHz and 27.4 MHz, is employed for applications like payment systems and library management. UHF RFID utilizes frequencies between 860 MHz and 960 MHz, enabling long-range reading, making it suitable for inventory management and supply chain tracking. MW RFID operates within the microwave frequency range of 2.45 GHz and is utilized in applications such as contactless payment systems and toll collection.</p></p>
