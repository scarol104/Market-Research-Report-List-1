<p><p>The plastic and chemical robotics market consists of various types of robots used in the plastic and chemical industries. Cartesian robots are three-axis robots with linear movements, suitable for precise and repetitive operations. SCARA robots are four-axis robots designed for fast and accurate horizontal movements. These robots contribute to automation, efficiency, and improved productivity in the plastic and chemical sectors.</p></p>
