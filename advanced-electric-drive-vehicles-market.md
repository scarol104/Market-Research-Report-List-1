<p><p>The Advanced Electric Drive Vehicles market can be categorized into four types. Hybrid Electric Vehicles (HEVs) combine an internal combustion engine with an electric motor to increase fuel efficiency and reduce emissions. Plug-In Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs) have a larger battery that can be charged from an external power source, allowing for all-electric driving for short distances. All-Electric Vehicles (EVs) run entirely on electric power and have no internal combustion engine. Fuel Cell Vehicles (FCVs) use hydrogen fuel cells to generate electricity and power an electric motor, emitting only water vapor.</p></p>
