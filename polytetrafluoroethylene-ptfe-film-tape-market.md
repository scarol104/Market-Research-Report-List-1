Standard-density PTFE film tape offers a balanced combination of flexibility and strength, making it ideal for general purposes. Medium-density PTFE film tape provides enhanced strength and durability, making it suitable for applications requiring more robust performance. High-density PTFE film tape has the highest strength and rigidity, making it suitable for applications where maximum mechanical strength is needed.</p></p>
