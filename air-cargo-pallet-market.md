<p><p>The Air Cargo Pallet market consists of two main types: Main Deck Pallet and Lower Deck Pallet. The Main Deck Pallet refers to larger pallets primarily used on the main deck of wide-body aircraft. These pallets have a higher weight capacity and are used to transport larger and heavier cargo. On the other hand, Lower Deck Pallets are smaller and designed to fit into the lower compartments of narrow-body and wide-body aircraft. These pallets are used to transport smaller cargo and are more commonly used in the air cargo industry.</p></p>
