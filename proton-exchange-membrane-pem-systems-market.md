<p><p>The Proton Exchange Membrane (PEM) Systems Market includes two main types of membranes: Polyaromatic Polymers Membrane and Partially Fluorinated Polymers Membrane. Polyaromatic Polymers Membrane is a type of membrane that is made from a class of polymers containing aromatic rings. On the other hand, Partially Fluorinated Polymers Membrane is a type of membrane that contains a mix of fluorine and non-fluorine atoms in its polymer structure. Both types are used in PEM systems to facilitate the transfer of protons while preventing the crossover of other gases or liquids.</p></p>
