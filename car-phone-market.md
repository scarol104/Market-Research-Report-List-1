<p><p>The car phone market is divided into two main types based on the network technology: CDMA (Code Division Multiple Access) and GSM (Global System for Mobile communications). CDMA networks use a spread-spectrum technique to transmit signals, allowing multiple users to share the same frequency simultaneously. GSM networks, on the other hand, utilize a time-division multiple access method, where each user is assigned a specific time slot for communication. Both CDMA and GSM offer different advantages and features, allowing consumers to choose the network type that best suits their needs and preferences for car phone connectivity.</p></p>
