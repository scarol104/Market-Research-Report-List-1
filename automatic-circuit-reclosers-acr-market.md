The first is Single Phase Recloser, which is suitable for single-phase electrical systems and protects against faults in one phase. The second type is Three Phase Recloser, designed for three-phase systems and provides protection against faults in all three phases. Lastly, the Triple Single Recloser is a combination of single and three-phase reclosers, offering comprehensive protection for both types of electrical systems.</p></p>
