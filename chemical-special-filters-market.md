<p><p>Chemical special filters are used to remove specific contaminants from various substances. Two common types of these filters are activated carbon filters and ion-exchange resin filters. Activated carbon filters work by adsorbing organic compounds and certain gases, improving the taste and smell of water and air. Ion-exchange resin filters operate by replacing unwanted ions in a solution with desired ones, enhancing water quality. Both filters are effective in removing specific pollutants, making them useful for various applications such as water and air purification.</p></p>
