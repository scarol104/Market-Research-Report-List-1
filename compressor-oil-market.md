<p><p>Compressor oil is categorized into two main types: common compressor oils and refrigeration compressor oils. Common compressor oils are used in various industrial applications to lubricate and cool compressors. These oils are suitable for compressors operating at moderate temperatures and pressures. On the other hand, refrigeration compressor oils are specifically formulated for use in refrigeration systems. These oils have low pour points and excellent thermal stability to ensure smooth operation of compressors in refrigeration applications. These two types of compressor oils cater to different industries and play a vital role in maintaining efficient compressor performance.</p></p>
