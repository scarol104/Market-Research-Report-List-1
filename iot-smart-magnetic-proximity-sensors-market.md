<p><p>The IoT smart magnetic proximity sensors market is categorized into various types based on their Ingress Protection (IP) rating. The IP00 sensors have no protection against ingress of solid or liquid, making them suitable for indoor use only. IP01 sensors provide protection against solid objects >50mm but no protection against liquid. IP02 sensors protect against solid objects >12.5mm and provide limited protection against water droplets. IP03 sensors offer protection against solid objects >2.5mm and limited protection against spraying water. The "Others" category includes sensors with different IP ratings not mentioned above, catering to specific requirements and environments.</p></p>
