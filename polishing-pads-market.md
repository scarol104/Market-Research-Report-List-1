<p><p>Polishing pads are divided into two main types: hard polishing pads and soft polishing pads. Hard polishing pads are used for aggressive polishing tasks that require the removal of deep scratches or tough blemishes on surfaces. They are made of a more rigid material that provides increased cutting power. On the other hand, soft polishing pads are designed for light polishing and finishing jobs. They have a more flexible composition that allows for a gentle and smooth polishing action, ideal for achieving a high-gloss shine on delicate surfaces.</p></p>
