<p><p>Boiler water treatment chemicals are used to prevent various issues that can occur in boiler systems. Corrosion inhibitors are chemicals that prevent metal corrosion by forming a protective layer on the inner surface of boilers. Scale inhibitors help in preventing the precipitation of mineral scales that can reduce heat transfer efficiency. Oxygen scavengers remove dissolved oxygen, preventing corrosion and pitting. Other types of chemicals may include pH adjusters, antifoaming agents, and biocides for the control of microorganisms. These chemicals play a crucial role in maintaining boiler efficiency and prolonging its lifespan.</p></p>
