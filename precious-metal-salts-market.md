<p><p>The precious metal salts market can be categorized into different types, including Ag salts (silver salts), Au salts (gold salts), PGMs salts (platinum group metals salts), and others. Similarly, Au salts are compounds containing gold, used primarily in jewelry, electronics, and decorative applications. PGMs salts encompass salts of platinum, palladium, and other related metals, commonly used in catalytic converters, fuel cells, and jewelry.
