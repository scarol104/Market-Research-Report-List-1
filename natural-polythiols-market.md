<p><p>The Natural Polythiols Market includes different types of polythiols, namely general type polythiols and pre-catalyzed polythiols. General type polythiols are versatile polymeric compounds that possess thiol (-SH) functional groups, making them suitable for various applications such as sealants, adhesives, coatings, and additives. Pre-catalyzed polythiols, on the other hand, are specifically designed with pre-activated catalysts, enabling enhanced curing properties. Both types cater to different market demands, offering a wide range of options for industries looking to utilize polythiols in their products.</p></p>
