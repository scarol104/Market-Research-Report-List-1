<p><p>Pyrogallic acid, also known as benzene-1,2,3-triol or pyrogallol, is a phenolic compound with the formula C6H3(OH)3. Pyrogallic acid is a white crystalline solid that has applications in various industries such as photography, pharmaceuticals, and agriculture. </p><p>In the photography industry, pyrogallic acid is used as a developing agent for black and white films and prints. It is also used in hair dyes and cosmetics due to its oxidizing properties. In the pharmaceutical industry, pyrogallic acid is used in the production of certain drugs, antiseptics, and topical creams. It is also used as an antioxidant in the food industry to prevent oxidative spoilage of some food products.</p><p>According to market analysis, the pyrogallic acid market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.2% during the forecast period.
