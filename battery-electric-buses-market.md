<p><p>Battery-Electric Buses (BEBs) are becoming popular as a sustainable transportation choice. There are three types of BEBs based on their power sources: Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs), Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs), and Fuel Cell Electric Vehicles (FCEVs). BEVs are fully electric buses powered solely by rechargeable batteries, providing zero emissions. PHEVs combine battery power with an internal combustion engine, allowing longer range capabilities. FCEVs use hydrogen fuel cells to generate electricity, emitting only water vapor. All three types contribute to reducing carbon emissions and promoting a greener transportation future.</p></p>
