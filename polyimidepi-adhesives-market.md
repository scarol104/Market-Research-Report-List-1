<p><p>Polyimide (PI) adhesives are available in two types: single-component and two-component. </p><p>Single-component PI adhesives are ready to use, meaning they do not require any additional mixing or preparation before application. These adhesives are often preferred due to their convenience and ease of use. They can be directly applied to bond materials together, eliminating the need for complex processes.</p><p>On the other hand, two-component PI adhesives consist of two separate components that need to be mixed together before application. These adhesives offer greater flexibility in terms of customization, as the mixing ratio can be adjusted to achieve specific adhesive performance requirements. However, they require additional preparation time and careful mixing to ensure proper bonding.</p></p>
