<p><p>The electric vehicle wireless charging market can be categorized into different types based on the technology used. Inductive technology utilizes an electromagnetic field to transfer energy between a charging pad on the ground and a receiver on the vehicle. Radiation technology, on the other hand, uses radio waves or microwaves to transmit power without direct physical contact. Lastly, the "Others" category refers to alternative technologies such as laser beams or resonant inductive coupling. These different types of wireless charging technologies offer various benefits and are being explored and developed for use in electric vehicles.</p></p>
