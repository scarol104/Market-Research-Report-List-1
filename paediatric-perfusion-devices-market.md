<p><p>The pediatric perfusion devices market includes various types of devices used in pediatric cardiac surgeries. Pediatric oxygenation systems provide oxygen and remove carbon dioxide from the blood during surgery. Reservoirs store and regulate blood flow during cardiopulmonary bypass procedures. Myocardial protection products help protect the heart from damage. Pediatric extracorporeal circuits support oxygenation and blood flow outside the body. Pediatric centrifugal blood pumps help circulate blood during surgery. Arterial line filters remove air, debris, and clots from the blood. Pediatric hemoconcentrators remove excess fluid and concentrate the blood. Pediatric suction and support devices assist with suction and support for the surgical procedure.</p></p>
