<p><strong>The Soft Touch Lamination Films Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One-Side Lamination Film</li><li>Double-Side Lamination Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The soft touch lamination films market consists of two main types: One-Side Lamination Film and Double-Side Lamination Film. One-Side Lamination Film is designed to be laminated on one side of a substrate, providing a soft and smooth texture. It is commonly used in applications like book covers or packaging materials. On the other hand, Double-Side Lamination Film is meant to be laminated on both sides of a substrate, offering a soft touch experience on both surfaces. This type is often utilized in high-end products such as luxury packaging or promotional materials.</p></p>
