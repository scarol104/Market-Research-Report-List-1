<p><p>PPS compounds are a type of material that consists of polyphenylene sulfide resin blended with various fillers to enhance their properties. There are different market types of PPS compounds available. </p><p>Glass Fiber Reinforced PPS compounds incorporate glass fibers into the resin, which improves their strength, stiffness, and heat resistance. These compounds are widely used in the automotive, electrical, and industrial sectors.</p><p>Glass Fiber & Mineral Filled PPS compounds combine both glass fibers and mineral fillers like silica or alumina. These compounds offer a balanced combination of mechanical strength, thermal stability, and dimensional stability, suitable for applications in electronics, aerospace, and consumer goods.</p><p>Other markets refer to specific applications where PPS compounds are customized to meet unique requirements, such as PPS compounds designed for high flame retardancy, chemical resistance, or wear resistance. These specialized compounds cater to industries like telecommunications, oil and gas, and medical devices.</p></p>
