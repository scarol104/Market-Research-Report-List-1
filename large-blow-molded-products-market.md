<p><p>The large blow molded products market consists of three main types: extrusion blow molding, injection blow molding, and stretch blow molding. </p><p>Extrusion blow molding involves melting plastic and forming it into a hollow tube, which is then inflated to the desired shape using compressed air. </p><p>Injection blow molding utilizes a preformed plastic tube or parison that is injected with molten plastic, creating a hollow product.</p><p>Stretch blow molding begins with a preformed preform that is stretched in both length and diameter, resulting in a thinner and lighter product.</p></p>
