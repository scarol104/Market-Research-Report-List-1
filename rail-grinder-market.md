<p><p>Rail grinders are specialized machines used for the maintenance and repair of railway tracks. There are different types of rail grinders, catering to specific market needs. Switch grinders are specifically designed for grinding the track switches, which are crucial for directing trains from one track to another. Main line grinders, on the other hand, are used for grinding the main lines of railway tracks. Both types serve their respective purposes in ensuring smooth and efficient railway operations, improving safety and reducing wear and tear on the tracks.</p></p>
