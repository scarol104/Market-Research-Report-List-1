<p><ul><li>Carbon Fiber</li><li>Epoxy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tow prepreg market consists of two main types: carbon fiber and epoxy. Carbon fiber prepreg is made by impregnating carbon fibers with a thermoset resin, resulting in a strong and lightweight material that is commonly used in aerospace, automotive, and sporting goods industries. Epoxy prepreg, on the other hand, uses epoxy resin to impregnate fibers such as glass, carbon, or aramid, providing high strength and durability for applications in construction, electronics, and marine industries. Both types offer unique properties and are widely utilized in various sectors due to their excellent performance characteristics.</p></p>
