<p><p>Loading dock shelters are used to create a seal between a truck and a loading dock, ensuring protection from external elements. Mechanical dock shelters employ a rigid frame and offer excellent insulation, durability, and ease of operation. Inflatable dock shelters, on the other hand, use airbags or curtains to form a seal and provide a flexible solution for various truck sizes and configurations. The "Others" category includes alternative types such as retractable dock shelters or hybrid models that combine elements of both mechanical and inflatable shelters.</p></p>
