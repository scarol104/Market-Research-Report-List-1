<p><p>The Heavy Duty Loading Ramps market consists of two main types: Hydraulic and Pneumatic. Hydraulic loading ramps operate using a hydraulic system that allows for easy lifting and lowering of heavy loads. This type of loading ramp is often used in industries where frequent loading and unloading of heavy equipment is required. On the other hand, Pneumatic loading ramps use a pneumatic system powered by compressed air to lift and lower loads. These ramps are typically used in situations where a more controlled and precise lifting process is necessary.</p></p>
