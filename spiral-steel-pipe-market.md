<p><p>Spiral steel pipes, also known as spiral submerged arc welded pipes (SSAW), are a type of steel pipe with a spiral seam running through its entire length. These pipes are manufactured by bending the steel strip or plate into a spiral shape and welding the seam using the submerged arc welding process. Spiral steel pipes are used in various applications such as water transmission, oil and gas pipelines, and structural piling.</p><p>The future outlook of the spiral steel pipe market looks promising. The growing demand for oil and gas transportation in emerging economies, rapid urbanization, and industrialization are some of the key factors driving the market growth.
