<p><p>Fibre Reinforced Readymix Concrete serves various purposes in construction projects. Transit Mixed Concrete refers to the concrete mixture that is prepared at a central plant and then transported to the construction site, where it is mixed again before usage. Shrink Mixed Concrete is partially mixed at the plant and then loaded into a truck for transportation to the construction site, where it is further mixed with water. Central Mixed Concrete is fully mixed at a central plant and then transported to the construction site, ready for immediate use.</p></p>
