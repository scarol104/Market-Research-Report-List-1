<p><p>The paper packaging tape market consists of two types: adhesive paper packaging tape and non-adhesive paper packaging tape. Adhesive paper packaging tape is commonly used for sealing boxes and cartons, as it has a strong adhesive backing that securely holds them together. Non-adhesive paper packaging tape, on the other hand, does not have adhesive properties and is typically used for bundling items or wrapping lightweight packages where a strong seal is not necessary. Both types of paper packaging tape play a crucial role in the packaging industry.</p></p>
