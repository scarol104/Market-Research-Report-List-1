<p><p>A Continuous Passive Motion (CPM) device is a medical equipment used in post-surgical rehabilitation to aid in the healing process of the joints, particularly the knee, elbow, and shoulder. It is designed to passively move the joint through a controlled range of motion, thereby preventing stiffness, reducing pain, and promoting the healing of tissues. CPM devices are extensively used in orthopedic clinics, rehabilitation centers, and hospitals.</p><p>The future outlook of the Continuous Passive Motion Device Market is expected to be promising. Factors such as the growing aging population, increasing prevalence of joint-related disorders and surgeries, and rising awareness about the benefits of CPM therapy are driving the market growth. Additionally, advancements in technology have led to the development of more efficient and comfortable CPM devices, further boosting the market.</p><p>Furthermore, the current outlook of the market is also positive. The market has witnessed significant growth in recent years, and this trend is expected to continue in the forecasted period. The growing demand for minimally invasive surgical procedures, the rise in healthcare expenditure, and the increasing adoption of CPM devices in home-based settings are contributing to the market's growth.</p><p>However, there are certain challenges that may hinder the market's growth, including the high cost of CPM devices, limited reimbursement policies, and the availability of alternative rehabilitation therapies. These factors may restrain the market, particularly in developing regions.</p><p>In conclusion, the Continuous Passive Motion Device Market is expected to experience steady growth in the coming years.
