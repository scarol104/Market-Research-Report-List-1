<p><p>The Outdoor Security Lighting market is categorized into three main types: motion sensor security lighting, solar security lighting, and hardwired security lighting. Motion sensor security lighting utilizes sensors to detect motion and turns on the lights as a response, serving as a deterrent against intruders. Solar security lighting harnesses the power of the sun to illuminate outdoor areas, making them energy-efficient and environmentally friendly. Hardwired security lighting, on the other hand, is directly connected to an electrical power source, providing a reliable and constant source of light for enhanced security.</p></p>
