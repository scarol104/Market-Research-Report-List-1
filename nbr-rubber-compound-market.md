<p><p>The NBR Rubber Compound Market comprises three major types: Block Type, Particles/Crumb Type, and Powder Type. Particles/Crumb Type consists of small particles or crumbs of NBR rubber compound, which are mainly utilized in applications like flooring, adhesives, and automotive components. Powder Type refers to finely ground NBR rubber compound, often used in coatings, paints, and oil-resistant products.
