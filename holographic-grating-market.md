<p><p>The Holographic Grating Market consists of two main types: Plane Type Holographic Grating and Concave Type Holographic Grating. Plane Type Holographic Gratings are essentially flat surfaces with a periodic structure that can manipulate light by diffracting it into various directions. They are commonly used in spectrometers and scientific instruments. On the other hand, Concave Type Holographic Gratings have a curved surface, allowing for better focus and dispersion of light. These gratings find applications in lasers, monochromators, and other optical devices. Both types play crucial roles in various industries and scientific research.</p></p>
