<p><p>Polyfilm is a versatile material used in various industries. BoPP and CPP are biaxially-oriented polypropylene and cast polypropylene, respectively, known for their clarity and moisture resistance. PVC is a rigid vinyl film used for packaging and construction. BoPET is a polyester film with high durability, and BoPA is a polyamide film known for its puncture resistance. "Others" encompass various polyfilm types like polystyrene, polyvinylidene chloride, etc.</p></p>
