<p><p>The in-ceiling speaker market consists of two main types: active in-ceiling speakers and passive in-ceiling speakers. Active in-ceiling speakers have built-in amplifiers, allowing them to receive a direct audio signal and produce sound independently. These speakers are easy to install and require minimal additional equipment. On the other hand, passive in-ceiling speakers do not have built-in amplifiers and require an external amplifier to receive and produce sound. They offer flexibility in terms of audio customization but require additional equipment and wiring for installation.</p></p>
