<p><p>The silicone elastomer market comprises various types of elastomers based on their composition and properties. Liquid silicone rubber (LSR) is a two-part platinum-cured elastomer, characterized by excellent fluidity and high thermal stability. High consistency rubber (HCR) is a solid silicone rubber, which offers high tear strength and compression set resistance. Fluorosilicone rubber (FSR) is a specialty elastomer with oil, fuel, and high-temperature resistance. Lastly, "Others" segment includes silicone blends and specialty elastomers used for specific applications, with unique properties like electrical conductivity or flame resistance.</p></p>
