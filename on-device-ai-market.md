<p><p>On-device AI refers to artificial intelligence technology that operates locally on a device, such as a smartphone, rather than relying on cloud-based processing. This technology has gained significant popularity due to its ability to provide faster and more secure AI solutions.</p><p>Market analysis predicts a steady growth for the On-device AI market. Factors driving this growth include the increasing demand for edge computing capabilities, growing concerns over data privacy and security, and the rising adoption of AI-powered applications. The COVID-19 pandemic has also accelerated the adoption of On-device AI as businesses seek to enhance remote working capabilities, improve efficiency, and limit reliance on cloud-based services for AI.</p><p>The market is witnessing a surge in demand for AI-enabled smartphones, wearable devices, and smart home appliances. On-device AI technology enables these devices to provide personalized user experiences, advanced voice recognition, real-time translation, image recognition, and augmented reality applications, among others. These advancements allow for more complex AI tasks to be performed locally, reducing the need for cloud-based processing and enhancing privacy.</p><p>Overall, the On-device AI market is expected to continue growing at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 7.2% during the forecast period.
